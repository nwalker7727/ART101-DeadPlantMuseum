Peace Lily

  • Lightly water when the top inch of soil feels dry.
  • They'll handle most light levels, but prefer indirect light.
  • Keep your peace lily hydrated in light, steamy bathrooms and kitchens.
  • Repot in spring using houseplant compost.

Cordatum

  • Water when soil has dried halfway through the pot.
  • Suitable for low and medium light spaces.
  • A good indication of your plant needing water is when the foliage begins to wilt and curl.

Fuschia

  • Deeply water your plants once a week.
  • Keep in an area with full direct sunlight.
  • Use micronutrient-rich plant fertilizer.
  • Trim the plant after the bloom starts to wilt to help new blooms grow.

Jalapeno Pepper Plant

  • Consistent moisture is essential.
  • Require more frequent watering during hot weather.
  • Wilting leaves are a sign it needs to be watered.

About Us

Here at the Dead Plant Museum, we combine nature with the macabre, as we explore causes of plant death and a large variety of plant diseases. Our founder, Sir Deadplantsalot, found interest in this topic at a young age after accidentally killing a series of succulents in succession. Our mission is to educate the public about ways to prevent untimely plant deaths while also acknoledging the fascinating aspects of the diseases that plague them.
